## Questions

### 1. What activity happens during the pre-processing stage of speech recognition?

- The audio is converted to .wmv format.
- Background noise is added to the audio signal.
- **Correct:** Feature vectors are extracted from the audio waveform for modeling.

### 2. What are phonemes?

- Artifacts that are removed from the signal as part of the clean-up process.
- **Correct:** The smallest unit of sound in speech.
- AI models that generate audio.

### 3. Why is it important to generate prosody in speech synthesis?

- Prosody maximizes the volume of the audio output.
- Prosody translates the speech to the language of the listener.
- **Correct:** Prosody ensures natural pronunciation and speech cadence.
