Guidelines:
- The maximum limit for flights is $600 per flight.
- The maximum limit for meals is $90 per meal, including breakfast, lunch, and dinner.
- The maximum limit for taxis and Rideshares is $75 per taxi or rideshare.
- The maximum limit for hotels is $200 for each night in a hotel.
- The maximum limit for other miscellaneous expenses is $50 per day.

To submit a claim for reimbursement, send an email to expenses@contoso.com with scanned receipts attached.
